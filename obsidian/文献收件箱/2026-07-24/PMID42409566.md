**原文：**A bioactive lysate of Corynebacterium glutamicum orchestrates intestinal barrier repair mainly through L-arginine-mediated FOXO pathway.
**来源：**PubMed → Food Res Int
**PubMed：**[PMID:42409566](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42409566)
**链接：**[DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.foodres.2026.119630)

## 核心发现
谷氨酸棒杆菌裂解物通过L-精氨酸激活FOXO通路，促进肠道屏障修复。具体数据未提供。

相关，涉及肠道屏障功能，与肠道菌群和营养健康相关。
