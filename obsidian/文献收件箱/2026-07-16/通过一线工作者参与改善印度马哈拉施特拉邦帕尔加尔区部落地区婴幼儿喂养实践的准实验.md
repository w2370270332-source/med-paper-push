**原文：**Protocol for a quasi experimental mixed method study on impact of intervention for improving Infant and Young Child Feeding (IYCF) practices in tribal block of Palghar District, Maharashtra, India through involvement of frontline workers
**来源：**PLOS ONE RSS
**链接：**[DOI](https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0353241)

## 核心发现
该方案旨在通过一线工作者干预改善婴幼儿喂养实践，具体效果待评估。

## 与研究方向关联
直接涉及婴幼儿喂养（营养）干预，与营养流行病学和预防医学相关。
