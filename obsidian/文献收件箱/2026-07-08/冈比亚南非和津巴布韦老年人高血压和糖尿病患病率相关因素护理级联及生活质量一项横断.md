**原文：**Hypertension and diabetes prevalence, associated factors, care cascade, and quality of life in older adults: A cross-sectional population-based study in The Gambia, South Africa, and Zimbabwe
**来源：**PLOS Medicine RSS
**链接：**[DOI](https://journals.plos.org/plosmedicine/article?id=10.1371/journal.pmed.1004785)

## 核心发现
该研究调查了三个非洲国家老年人高血压和糖尿病的患病率、相关因素、护理级联及生活质量，提供了流行病学数据。

## 与研究方向关联
涉及慢性病（高血压、糖尿病）流行病学，与慢性病预防领域相关。
